Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

ORACLE ("we," "our," or "the app") is an AI-powered sports prediction and analytics tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application and website at www.oraclepredictapp.com.

Information We Collect

ORACLE is designed with privacy in mind. We do not require user accounts, login credentials, or personal information to use the app.

Information we do NOT collect:

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, location data, contacts, photos, or any other personal information. We do not require account creation or login.

Information that may be automatically collected:

Like most web applications, our hosting provider (Railway) may automatically log basic server access information such as IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used solely for maintaining service stability and is not used for tracking, advertising, or profiling.

Local Storage

ORACLE stores certain preferences and data locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes your bet tracker history, custom alert settings, and UI preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

ORACLE uses the following third-party services to provide sports data:

• The Odds API — for sportsbook odds and lines
• ESPN — for scores, standings, and player statistics
• PandaScore — for esports data

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share any user data with these services; we only receive sports data from them.

Advertising & Tracking

ORACLE does not display advertisements. We do not use any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, analytics services, or third-party cookies. We do not track your behavior across websites or apps.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any personal information with third parties. We do not engage in data brokering or user profiling.

Children's Privacy

ORACLE is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Gambling Disclaimer

ORACLE is for entertainment and research purposes only. The app does not facilitate gambling, accept wagers, or process any financial transactions. No bets can be placed through this app. Users are encouraged to gamble responsibly and in accordance with local laws.

Data Security

All connections to ORACLE are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal risk of data exposure.
